About Me

I am a 4th-year student at UVA interested in Low-level programming and Machine Learning. I have developed hands-on experience with Compilers, Binary Exploits, and Deep/Machine Learning using TensorFlow, Keras, and Numpy. Additionally, I have industry experience with fullstack development using React and C#, and cloud development using Azure services.

I am most passionate about computer vision/perception, navigation and path planning in robotics and autonomous vehicles. I have extensive experience working with stereo-depth cameras such as Intel RealSense solutions and OpenCV through my involvement in UVA's Mechatronics and Robotics Society (MARS).
